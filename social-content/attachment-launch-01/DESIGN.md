# Northstar Short-Form Video Identity

## Style Prompt

Premium dark editorial motion derived from Northstar's Antigravity interface: ink-black space, blue-violet glass panels, crisp white typography, restrained celestial particles, and decisive interface-like labels. The tone should feel private, intelligent, emotionally perceptive, and commercially polished rather than mystical or clinical.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#090B12`
- Glass panel: `#141925`
- Primary text: `#F4F6FB`
- Secondary text: `#A8B0C2`
- Accent blue: `#5B8CFF`
- Accent violet: `#9A7CFF`

## Typography

- Display statements: `Archivo Black`
- Supporting and product copy: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Motion

- Medium-fast editorial entrances with varied direction and scale.
- Blur crossfades between related thoughts.
- Subtle ambient drift only in background particles and orbital lines.
- Hold key statements long enough to read without audio.

## What NOT to Do

- No rainbow gradients or generic neon cyberpunk effects.
- No astrology glyph clutter, crystal imagery, or fortune-teller styling.
- No dense paragraphs or tiny web-sized text.
- No claims that diagnose the viewer or guarantee relationship outcomes.
- No aggressive flashing, jump cuts, or high-frequency glitch effects.
